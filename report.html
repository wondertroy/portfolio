TMPMRP

Tricky Multi-Player Multi-Reality Puzzle


Woody Hill

Building Teamwork and communication skills

The TMPMRP aims to get people talking to each other in a collaborative problem solving setting. Participants need to be able to clearly and concisely share their ideas with each other in order to succeed.



Levels are designed so that no one person can know how to complete them without talking to their team mate. Levels also include components which physically require multiple participants to get past.

Technology

The TMPMRP is a Virtual Reality experience built in unity. The HTC VIVE is used to accurately track the cube positions and displays the virtual puzzle in the headset.
